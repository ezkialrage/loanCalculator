Welcome to The Loan Repayment Plan. This site was born out of necessity and will assist you in planning for your future. A lot of the student loan calculators don't focus on the "Repayment" aspect of loans; they usually just tell you how much you need to take out to finish college. What about if you're already in repayment? That's where TLRP comes in.

The main goal of this site was to help college students get a gauge for how long they will be in debt once they start paying their loans off, but the tools can be used for anyone with any kind of debt (student loans, home loans, bank loans ect...). To get started, enter your remaining loan amount, interest rate and how much you plan to spend each month and TLRP will do the rest!
